The project employs a **Brutalist Editorial** aesthetic, inspired by independent fanzines and avant-garde magazines from the 50s and 90s.
- **Typography**: The contrast between the expressiveness of the `Syne` typeface (headings) and the technical structure of `Space Grotesk` (body text).
- **Colors**: A minimalist palette based on organic tones (`#FAF3E1`) with vibrant orange accents (`#FA8112`).
- **Interaction**: Intentional "viscous" micro-animations, such as the pulsing circle, designed to create a physical sensation of "fitting" and focus.
